Grandma Recipes

There are many recipes of grandmother which are still very effective in enhancing our immunity. For example- Turmeric Milk, Orange Juice with Peeper, Tulsi-Peepercorn, Immunity Balls, Ginger and Tulsi Juice, Kadha, Amla Juice, Neem Juice, Immunity Boosting Tomato Soup.

We all know that the combination of milk and turmeric is always good for boosting the immune system. During this viral season, make sure that you add have half a teaspoon of turmeric in one cup of boiled milk. Mix well and drink hot. It is suggested to consume milk at night 20-30 minutes before going to bed.

Drink a glass of fresh orange juice daily to which a pinch of pepper has been added. It is loaded with antioxidants and is a rich source of Vitamin C. It will naturally help in boosting your immunity.

Begin your day with home grown tulsi leaves along with organic honey and freshly crushed peppercorn. Take 5-7 leaves, add two crushed peppercorns with one teaspoon honey and consume immediately on an empty stomach and do not drink water after this.

Take one teaspoon powdered turmeric, 1 tsp jaggery, 1 tsp cow ghee and 1 tsp dry ginger powder. Mix well and make into small round balls. Have 2-3 daily.

To make Ginger Tulsi Juice All you need to do is, take juice of fresh ginger, crush some tulsi leaves in it. Add a teaspoon of honey to it. Consume it daily to get relief from cough and increase the body immunity.

Make a warm tea with few tulsi leaves, a piece of ginger and a dash of black pepper. All these ingredients play an important role in fighting illness-causing bacteria and increase the body's immunity.

Amla juice is a powerhouse of nutrition and is matchless in its power to boost the body's immunity. Have half a teaspoon of crushed fresh amla with 1 crushed garlic clove on an empty stomach.
